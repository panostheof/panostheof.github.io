The competition "F1 in Schools" takes place in more than 30 countries involving more than 17,000 students worldwide. Through the” F1 in Schools” we gained Innovation & Entrepreneurship knowledge, learned to cooperate on standards of professional teams of Formula 1 and this led us to the empirical acquisition of basic knowledge on Science, New Technologies, Mechanics and Mathematics (STEM Education ), and the development of valuable skills and competences (personal and professional).

At the same time, we comprehented concepts such as aerodynamic objects, the design and production of industrial products, and learned to design and implement business plan, to cope with projects, marketing and promotion, the idea of business as well as to understand the meaning of sponsorship and communication.
